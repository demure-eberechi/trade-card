Blue and Gold Macaw

The Blue and Gold Macaw typically lives from 30-35 years. They are a varlety of parrot which is a "talking bird". They eat nuts, seeds and fruits. Once paired, macaws mate for life. The females typically lay two or three eggs at a time. Macaws are very popular as pets because of their appearance and also their intelligence they can leam many tricks from their owners.

Malayan Tapir

The Malaysian Tapir has black and white hair to camouflage itself from predators; Its colors may make other animals think It is a large rock. They have three toes on their front feet and four on the back. Mother taples are pregnant from 390- 395 days before giving birth. Once born, babies have brown hair with white spots which changes color around 47 months. Tapirs are herbivores, warm blooded, and nocturnal.
